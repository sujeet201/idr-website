The IDR logo uses a geometric cube-inspired shape to represent structure, stability and resilience in digital systems.
 The orange colour represents innovation, energy and forward-looking technology, 
 while black and white provide strong contrast and a professional minimalist appearance. 
 The cube structure symbolises interconnected digital risk areas such as cyber security, 
 AI governance and technology risk management. 
 A clean sans-serif typography ensures readability and clarity across digital platforms including mobile headers and small icon sizes.
